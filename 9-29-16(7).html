What is InstantCheckmate.com?
InstantCheckmate.com provides you with the most useful, detailed and important information on just about anyone. Whether researching criminal history, phone numbers, addresses, demographic data, census data, or a wide variety of other information, we help thousands of Americans find what they're looking for each and every day. Back to Top

Where does Instant Checkmate get their information for reports?
InstantCheckmate.com compiles reports from millions of public records including information provided by state and local governments. All of the information contained in our reports is part of what is referred to as the "public record." Back to Top

Why isn't this service free?
InstantCheckMate.com compiles reports from millions of public records including information provided by state and local governments. Unlike most of our competitors, InstantCheckMate.com allows our members to make informed decisions about the people in their lives on a budget. While most background check providers charge a per report fee, InstantCheckMate.com members have the ability to perform unlimited searches for a low monthly rate. Back to Top

What does our Instant Checkmate BACKGROUND CHECK service provide?
Instant Checkmate’s Background Check service may provide information such as addresses, relatives, arrest records, personal information (date of birth, aliases), social profile information, horoscopes, nearby sex offenders, and more. Back to Top

What does our Instant Checkmate REVERSE PHONE LOOKUP service provide?
Instant Checkmate’s Reverse Phone Lookup service may provide information on a phone number that may include name (business or residential), owner history, current address, address history, satellite views, landline or cellular, cellular carrier, elevation of residence or business, estimated house value and income, and much more. Back to Top

What information comes in an Email report?
Instant Checkmate's Email Report contains, where available, a person’s name, address history, related persons, social media profiles including additional email addresses, and professional networking information. A subscription to Instant Checkmate's Email Reports allows you to obtain unlimited Email Reports about email addresses during your subscription period. Back to Top

What information comes in a Standard report?
Instant Checkmate's Standard Background Report contains, where available, a person’s date of birth, phone numbers, address history, related persons, arrest records, government license information, social media profiles including email addresses, and nearby sex offenders. A subscription to Instant Checkmate's Standard Background Report service allows you to obtain unlimited Standard Background Reports about persons during your subscription period. After subscribing, you will also have the opportunity to upgrade your Standard Report to a Premium Report, which will supply additional and supplemental data about a person, for an additional charge. Back to Top
